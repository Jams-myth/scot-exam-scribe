About Nat5 Study Platform

A comprehensive study support platform for Scottish National 5 exam preparation.

Our Mission

The Nat5 Study Platform is designed to help students prepare effectively for their Scottish National 5 exams by providing access to interactive practice questions extracted from official SQA exam papers.

Our goal is to make exam preparation more engaging, effective, and accessible for all students.

Core Features

PDF Content Extraction

We parse SQA Nat5 exam PDFs to extract questions, mark allocations, and marking guidelines, maintaining the hierarchical structure of exam papers.

Interactive Question Conversion

We transform extracted questions into interactive digital formats, preserving the original difficulty level and assessment criteria.

Assessment Engine

Our system evaluates student responses based on official SQA marking guidelines, providing specific feedback on incorrect or partially correct answers.

Learning Support

We implement help buttons for each question that provide progressive hints, subject-specific guidance, and step-by-step solution paths for complex problems.

How It Works

  1. Upload Exam Papers: Users can upload official SQA Nat5 exam papers in PDF format.
  2. Extract Questions: Our system extracts questions, mark allocations, and marking schemes from the PDFs.
  3. Practice Questions: Students can practice with interactive versions of the extracted questions.
  4. Receive Feedback: Students receive immediate feedback on their answers based on official marking schemes.
  5. Track Progress: The system tracks student progress and identifies areas for improvement.
